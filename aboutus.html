About Us
CoCA is a team of high-schoolers developing a walking cane that makes the everyday life of visually-impaired individuals easier by allowing them to remotely activate crosswalks
Rohith Yelisetty

Chief Executive Officer (CEO)

Hi! My name is Rohith and I am the Chief Executive Officer and co-founder of Connected Crosswalk Assistance (CoCA), and a student of the class of 2025 at TJHSST. The idea of CoCA was created after watching numerous visually-impaired individuals in my community struggle to cross the street. Therefore, along with Anirudh, Tanush, Arnav, and Adithiya, I came up with the idea of being able to remotely access and activate crosswalks from the push of a button. As a high schooler, our ideas are often shut down due to a lack of "experience" and "practicality", however, I wish to show everyone that with passion and perseverance, even our wildest dreams are achievable. As your CEO, my job is to ensure that all of the teams achieve our startup's goals which include the CAD of the walking cane, the development of our computer vision algorithm, the completion of our research papers, the securing of funds and grants, and obtaining a patent for our propietary technology.
